## Core Rule

**Color accuracy over brand — no default brand preference for metallics (updated 2026-06-13).**

Pick the metallic that best hits the target value and hue from whatever's in inventory. VMC is no longer mandated over Pro Acryl for airbrush metallics, and no brand is preferred by default.

**Scope: this is a global paint-selection rule — applies to ALL paints, not just metallics.** Exception: **Expert Acrylics** remains the special-case pick for brush work and glazing (extended open time + blendability — a medium behavior, not brand loyalty). Transparents, color-shift, and speedpaints are likewise chosen for finish/medium behavior.

**Why:** The prime directive is value/hue accuracy; locking a brand contradicts it. Brand only matters where a finish or medium genuinely behaves differently (transparents, color-shift, speedpaints) or where delivery method constrains the choice.

**How to apply:** Choose by the result you need. VMC's pre-thinned ultra-fine pigment still makes it a strong *practical* pick for fine airbrush work, and the tables below remain a useful reference for that — but treat them as options, not a rule. Pro Acryl metallics are equally valid wherever they hit the target.

## TMM — 1:6 Scale Gloss Black Base Method (VMC airbrush)

Airbrush steps override the general TMM section; brush steps unchanged.

1. **Gloss Black base coat** — Satin Black S39 + gloss medium, or dedicated gloss black. Glossy surface required.
2. **Map light/dark bands.**
3. **Mid-tone metallic (Airbrush)** — VMC 77.702 Duraluminium thinned 1:1 distilled water (not 1:3 — VMC is pre-thinned). Multiple thin passes.
4. **Brighter mid-tone (Airbrush)** — VMC 77.701 Aluminium over light-facing plane centers. Feather into Duraluminium.
5. **Bright edge highlight (Airbrush)** — VMC 77.707 Chrome only on apex edges/specular peaks. Very fine; reduce PSI, increase distance.
6. **Shadow/recess (optional):** FW Payne's Gray Ink lightly airbrushed into shadow planes (satin preserves reflectivity; not ideal for warm metals); or Black Wash 200 / Nuln Oil into deep mechanical recesses only.
7. **Colored metallics (Airbrush):** Copper → 77.710 over gloss black, shadow with Transparent Brown 052; Gold → 77.725, shadow with Transparent Brown 052 / Sepia; heat zones → layer 77.720 → 77.713 → 77.723.

## Thinning VMC for Airbrush

VMC is pre-thinned for 0.4mm nozzle:
- **0.5mm:** slightly undiluted to ~1:1 distilled water
- **0.4mm:** 1:1 paint:water sweet spot — do not over-thin
- **0.3mm:** 1:1.5 to 1:2 — test first; can spider if over-reduced
- **No Flow Improver needed** — VMC has very low surface tension
- **Agitate frequently** — pigment settles within minutes
